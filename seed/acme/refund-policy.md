# Refund Policy

Last updated: August 18, 2025

## Purpose

This policy defines the default response for outage-related refund and service-credit requests.

## Standard rules

1. Acme does not offer automatic cash refunds for service interruptions.
2. Service credits are the standard remedy unless contract language requires something else.
3. Credits up to 14 days may be approved by the customer success lead when outage impact is documented.
4. Credits from 15 to 30 days require finance approval.
5. Requests above 30 days should normally be declined as out of policy.

## Escalation notes

- Customer success should never promise a refund amount before finance review.
- Executive review may be requested for edge cases, but executive visibility does not override the need for finance sign-off.
- Incident duration should be measured from confirmed customer impact, not from the first internal alert.

## Example

If an outage lasts four hours and a customer asks for two months of fees back, the request should be escalated, documented, and expected to land below the requested amount.
